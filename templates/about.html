Who we are

Talent-Bridge is a skills development program that focuses on developing the capabilities that the market is looking for. We work with individuals looking to align their skills to the market needs and our program focuses on getting individuals Proficient, Opportunity Ready and Globally Competitive

As an African start-up we want to write our future and fully live the present day. We believe Africa is the “Next Frontier” and we can change our story from the “Dark continent” to what each African positively aspires it to be. We don’t just talk we do, every day we take steps that draw us closer to our goal. Africa doesn’t have a talent problem its just not in circulation.


Our Mission

To tap into the talent of African youth and get them to understand, acquire enhance and incorporate the skills required for a swift transition into an evolving and globally competitive market.
