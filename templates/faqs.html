Frequently Asked Questions

What is Talent Bridge Africa?

Talent-Bridge is a skills development program that focuses on developing capabilities that the market is looking for

What informed the formation of Talent Bridge

Talent-Bridge was informed by the mismatch in skills that the workforce has versus the skills that employers are looking for. This gap has resulted in unemployment and underemployment while making it hard for companies to fill numerous open positions

What takes place during the program

During the program, you go through a comprehensive skills assessment, create a development plan to bridge the gaps and practice on internships or projects that give you work experience in the desired skills

Who is qualified to take the program

The program is open to anyone who meets our program fit check. This includes university/ college students, unemployed, underemployed and employed individuals who want to develop their skills

When does the program take place?

This is a self-paced online program that happens all year round, the skills development pace is dependent on the individual

Can I take the program while still employed?

Yes, this is a self-paced online program happens all year round, the skills development progression is dependent on the individual. However, your practice mode shall be on projects and not internships

Do I need to relocate / report to your offices to take the program?

The only part that requires you be physically present in a specifies location is when you are working on your internship/ project. There will be projects/ internships that will conducted remotely but this is dependent on the employer and will be fewer in number

What do i need to apply to the program?

The only part that requires you be physically present in a specifies location is when you are working on your internship/ project. There will be projects/ internships that will conducted remotely but this is dependent on the employer and will be fewer in number

What are the benefit at the end of program?

The benefits of the program include:

  • Skills assessment against a global standard
  • Continuous skills profiling against any career path
  • Work readiness training which includes customer service, communication, time management, working in a team etc
  • Access to online courses for learning and certification
  • Skills practice on either projects or internships
  • Self-paced and can be done from anywhere
  • Access to corporate mentors and industry experts
  • Enlisted into corporates recruitment pool
